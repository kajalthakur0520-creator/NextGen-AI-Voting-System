Introduction

  • A Pillar of Democracy: Voting allows citizens to choose leaders and actively participate in the nation's decision-making.
  • Key Requirements: To function properly, the voting process must be secure, transparent, fair, and absolutely trustworthy.
  • Overcoming Barriers: Traditional voting often limits participation due to geography, time constraints, and long physical lines.
  • Digital Transformation: Adapting democratic processes to the modern digital era is essential to increase voter turnout and efficiency.
  • The AI Advantage: Utilizing Artificial Intelligence brings unprecedented security to voter identity verification, creating a smarter platform.

Existing Problem

Traditional voting systems often face challenges that reduce public trust:

  • Fake Voting & Identity Theft: Risk of unauthorized proxy voting.
  • Manual Verification Delays: Slow processes and long queues.
  • Lack of Transparency: Voters cannot easily verify their casted votes.
  • Security Vulnerabilities: High risk of tampering and human error.

Proposed System

An AI-Powered Secure Web-Based Biometric Voting System

  • AI Face Verification: Biometric scanning to verify real voter identity.
  • Multi-Factor Auth: Uses Passwords, CAPTCHA, and OTP protection.
  • Encrypted Storage: Votes are securely stored with encryption and audit logs.
  • Dedicated Modules: Separate portals for Voters, Candidates, and Administrators.

Objectives of the System

✅ Develop a secure web-based voting system.
✅ Verify voter identity using AI face recognition.
✅ Implement multi-factor authentication.
✅ Prevent fake voting and duplicate voting.
✅ Securely store votes using encryption techniques.
✅ Reduce manual verification and human errors.
✅ Provide transparency through audit logs.
✅ Improve efficiency, speed, and reliability.

System Architecture

A modular architecture integrating AI and secure web systems:

  • The frontend communicates with the Flask backend server.
  • The backend processes requests and performs AI-based biometric verification.
  • Data is stored securely in the MongoDB database.
  • Features OTP authentication and encrypted vote storage to ensure security.

Core System Modules

👤 Voter Module

Allows users to register, log in securely, verify identity (Face + OTP), participate in elections, and submit complaints. Provides vote receipt generation.

🏛️ Candidate Module

Allows candidates to register by submitting party details, manifestos, and logos. Awaits administrator approval before participating.

⚙️ Admin Module

Manages the overall election. Verifies voters, approves candidates, creates elections, monitors complaints, and views audit logs.

Advantages of the System

  • ✅ Provides secure voting using multi-factor authentication.
  • ✅ Prevents fake voting and duplicate voting through biometric verification.
  • ✅ Uses AI-based face recognition for accurate voter identification.
  • ✅ Secures vote data using encryption techniques.
  • ✅ Reduces manual work and human errors in election management.
  • ✅ Improves transparency through audit logs and vote receipt generation.

Future Scope

Conclusion

The AI-Powered Secure Web-Based Biometric Voting System provides a secure, transparent, and efficient online voting platform by integrating biometric face verification, OTP authentication, and encrypted vote storage.

The system helps reduce fake voting, improves voter verification, and enhances election transparency and reliability. By combining artificial intelligence with modern web technologies, the proposed system offers a smarter and more secure approach to digital voting.
